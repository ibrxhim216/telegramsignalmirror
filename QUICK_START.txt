Welcome to Telegram Signal Mirror! Get started in 3 easy steps:


STEP 1: WEB PORTAL SETUP (5 minutes)
================================================================================

1. Visit: https://www.telegramsignalmirror.com

2. Create your account or login

3. Subscribe to a plan:
   - Basic: 1 MT4/MT5 account, 1 channel
   - Pro: 3 MT4/MT5 accounts, 3 channels
   - Lifetime: 3 MT4/MT5 accounts, 3 channels (one-time payment)

4. Register your MT4/MT5 trading accounts:
   - Click "Add Account" in the web portal
   - Enter your account number and select platform (MT4 or MT5)
   - Save

Your accounts are now registered in the cloud!


STEP 2: DESKTOP APP SETUP (3 minutes)
================================================================================

1. Double-click: "Telegram Signal Mirror 1.0.1.exe"

   Note: Windows may show a security warning. Click "More info" then "Run anyway"
   (This is normal for apps without expensive code signing certificates)

2. Login with your web portal credentials (same email/password)

3. Connect to Telegram:
   - Click "Connect to Telegram"
   - Enter your Telegram phone number (with country code, e.g., +1234567890)
   - Enter the verification code sent to your Telegram app
   - Click "Confirm"

4. Start Monitoring:
   - Click "Select Channels"
   - Choose which Telegram signal channels to monitor
   - Click "Start Monitoring"

The desktop app is now receiving signals and pushing them to the cloud!


STEP 3: MT4/MT5 EA SETUP (2 minutes)
================================================================================

FOR MT4:
--------
1. Copy "TelegramSignalMirror.ex4" to:
   C:\Program Files\[Your Broker MT4]\MQL4\Experts\

2. Open MT4
3. Open any chart (symbol doesn't matter)
4. Drag the EA "TelegramSignalMirror" onto the chart
5. In EA settings:
   - Set your Account Number (must match what you registered in web portal)
   - Enable "Allow live trading" and "Allow DLL imports"
   - Click OK

FOR MT5:
--------
1. Copy "TelegramSignalMirror.ex5" to:
   C:\Program Files\[Your Broker MT5]\MQL5\Experts\

2. Open MT5
3. Open any chart (symbol doesn't matter)
4. Drag the EA "TelegramSignalMirror" onto the chart
5. In EA settings:
   - Set your Account Number (must match what you registered in web portal)
   - Enable "Allow algo trading" and "Allow DLL imports"
   - Click OK


YOU'RE DONE!
================================================================================

The complete signal flow is now active:

Telegram Channel → Desktop App → Cloud → MT4/MT5 EAs → Automatic Trades


HOW IT WORKS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. When a signal is posted in your monitored Telegram channel
2. Desktop app receives it instantly and parses the signal
3. Desktop app pushes to cloud API
4. Cloud distributes to ALL your registered MT4/MT5 accounts
5. Each MT4/MT5 EA polls the cloud every 2 seconds
6. EA executes the trade automatically on your account

Multi-account support means ONE signal → MULTIPLE accounts execute!

TROUBLESHOOTING:
================================================================================
Q: Desktop app won't open?
A: Right-click → Properties → Unblock → Apply

Q: EA shows "Account not found"?
A: Verify account is registered in web portal with exact same number

Q: No trades executing?
A: Check MT4/MT5 Experts tab for error messages
   Ensure "Allow algo trading" is enabled

Q: Desktop app lost connection?
A: Click "Reconnect" - your settings are saved locally
